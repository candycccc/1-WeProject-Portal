ABOUT LCR INTEGRATED SYSTEMS

HOME AUTOMATION SYSTEMS DESIGNED TO OUR CLIENT’S LIFESTYLE

LCR Integrated Systems was founded by Lee Roche and his passion for cutting edge technology. With extensive prior experience in commercial electrical installations, including large scale wiring projects within banking establishments, Lee Roche applied his knowledge to home automation, to improve the way we interact with our space.

Today, we have grown to be one of the country’s leading experts in systems integration, providing electrical design and consultancy services to a range of projects around the UK. We are committed to giving our customers a personal, bespoke service, that makes the most of their environment and revolutionises how they use it.
